# Facebook Page Strategy: Cats & Dogs Niche

## 1. Page Positioning

### Recommended Page Names
- Cute Cats & Dogs Hub
- Paw Moments
- Happy Pets Daily
- Cat & Dog World
- Pet Love Stories
- Fluffy Friends Daily

Avoid complex names, emojis, or copied brand names.

### Page Category
- Pet Service (Recommended)
- Personal Blog
- Digital Creator

Best choice: Pet Service

### Page Bio
Daily cute cat and dog videos, heartwarming pet moments, funny animal clips, and adorable pet stories from around the world.

---

## 2. Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars

1. Cute & Emotional Moments
- Sleeping cats
- Puppies playing
- Rescue stories

2. Funny Pet Videos
- Cats scared of objects
- Dog reactions
- Unexpected pet behavior

3. Educational Content
- Pet care tips
- Training guides
- Nutrition advice

4. Viral Animal Clips
- Use only original or heavily edited/licensed content

---

## 3. 30-Day Posting Plan

### Daily Routine
- 3 Reels per day
- 1–2 photo posts
- 5–8 stories

### Week 1
Focus: Engagement setup
- Cute clips
- Simple captions

### Week 2
Focus: Viral testing
- Funny clips
- Reaction videos

### Week 3
Focus: Viral growth
- Emotional rescue stories
- Story-based reels

### Week 4
Focus: Authority
- Pet tips
- Educational posts

---

## 4. Viral Reel Formula

### Hook (0–2 sec)
- “Wait for it…”
- “This will melt your heart…”
- “Dogs react to this…”

### Visual (2–10 sec)
- Cute clips
- Fast cuts
- Close-up shots

### Emotion (10–20 sec)
- Reaction or story moment

### CTA
- Follow for more pets
- Save this video

---

## 5. Monetization Safety Rules

### Safe Content
- Original videos
- Licensed footage
- Edited compilations with commentary

### Avoid
- Reused TikTok videos
- Movie or TV clips
- Copyright compilations
- Spam repost pages

---

## 6. Growth Strategy

- Reply to comments
- Post stories daily
- Use hashtags:
  #cats #dogs #pets #cutepets #animalvideos

### Best Posting Times (Sri Lanka)
- Morning: 7–9 AM
- Evening: 6–10 PM
